# Code of Conduct — TwitchLift Community

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone interested in TwitchLift, the #1 Twitch view bot and viewer bot service.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Spam or unsolicited advertising
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the TwitchLift team:
- **Email:** support@twitchlift.com
- **Telegram:** [@twitchlift](https://t.me/twitchlift)

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
