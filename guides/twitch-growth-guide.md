## The Discoverability Problem

Twitch's directory system sorts channels by viewer count. Streams with 0-5 viewers are buried at the bottom of game categories, making it nearly impossible for new viewers to discover them organically. This creates a "chicken and egg" problem: you need viewers to get discovered, but you need to be discovered to get viewers.
