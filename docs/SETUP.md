# TwitchLift Setup Guide — Twitch View Bot Quick Start

Get started with TwitchLift, the #1 Twitch viewer bot service, in under 2 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A Twitch account with an active channel
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- That's it! No downloads, no proxies, no technical setup required.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [twitchlift.com/register](https://twitchlift.com/register)
2. Enter your email address and choose a password
3. Verify your email through the confirmation link
4. Log in to your new TwitchLift dashboard

> 💡 **Tip:** You can also sign in with Google OAuth for faster registration.

---

## Step 2: Try the Free Trial

Before purchasing, test our Twitch view bot for free:

1. From the dashboard, click **"Start Free Trial"**
2. Enter your Twitch channel name
3. Click **"Activate Trial"**
4. 500 viewers will appear on your stream within seconds
5. Trial lasts 10 minutes — plenty of time to verify it works!

> ⚠️ **Note:** Free trial is limited to one per account. No credit card required.

After your trial, pick a plan that fits your needs:

## Step 4: Start Your Campaign

Once your payment is confirmed:

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Click **"Start Boost"** next to your active plan
3. Enter your Twitch channel name
4. Optionally adjust the viewer count (in increments of 50)
5. Click **"Activate"**
6. Watch your viewer count rise in real-time!

## Advanced Configuration

### Custom Viewer Count
From the dashboard, use the viewer count input to set your exact target. Counts must be multiples of 50 and cannot exceed your plan maximum.

### Auto-Restart
TwitchLift automatically restarts viewers when your stream goes offline and comes back. No manual intervention needed.

### Multiple Channels
Purchase separate plans for each channel. Manage all channels from a single dashboard.

## Troubleshooting

### Viewers not appearing?
1. Make sure your Twitch stream is **live** and **public**
2. Check that your channel name is spelled correctly (case-insensitive)
3. Wait 30-60 seconds for initial viewer ramp-up
4. Contact support if issues persist

### Payment not processing?
1. Ensure you sent the **exact amount** shown on the checkout page
2. Crypto payments require network confirmations (typically 10-30 minutes)
3. Check your dashboard for payment status updates
4. Contact support with your order ID for manual verification

### Dashboard not loading?
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser or incognito/private mode
3. Check your internet connection
4. Contact support if the issue continues
